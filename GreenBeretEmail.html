Your most powerful weapon is your mind.

Each Soldier who dons the Green Beret, goes through a rigorous training program, which can take up to two years. Candidates are tried through physical and mental challenges that test not only their physical strength but also their character by a selection process that assesses them on core attributes. Special Forces candidates must possess: adaptability, perseverance, personal responsibility, team player, courage, capability, professionalism and integrity.
